Learning Management System

Location Of Vehicles On The Roadway

  • On two-way roads with four lanes or more, it is forbidden to exit on the side of the road intended for oncoming traffic.

  • Vehicles whose speed does not exceed 40 km / h or which, for technical reasons, do not reach such speed, move in the extreme right lane intended for driving this vehicle in the desired direction, except in cases of detour, overtaking, changing the line before turning left, turn or stop (parking) in permitted cases on the left side of the road.

  • When turning on the road with reverse movement, the driver drives the vehicle so that when leaving the intersection of the carriageways, the vehicle takes the right lane. Changing line is allowed only after the driver is satisfied that driving in this direction is allowed in other lanes.
